Our Story

A Partnership That Built a Nation

Debswana Diamond Company (Pty) Ltd was established in 1969 as a 50/50 joint venture between the Government of the Republic of Botswana and De Beers Group. Together, we have grown into one of the largest and most respected diamond producers in the world.

From the first discovery at Orapa, our work has helped transform Botswana from one of the world's poorest countries at independence into one of Africa's strongest economies — funding schools, hospitals, roads and the dreams of generations.
