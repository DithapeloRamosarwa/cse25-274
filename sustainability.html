Home · Sustainability
Our Commitment

People. Planet. Prosperity.

Sustainability isn't an add-on for Debswana — it's woven into how we mine, who we hire, and how we leave the land. Every diamond we sell helps fund schools, clinics, water access and biodiversity in Botswana.

We are committed to net-zero carbon by 2050, water positivity in all operations by 2030, and rehabilitation of every hectare we disturb.

Focus Areas

Where We Make a Difference

Wildlife Protection

Anti-poaching partnerships and conservation areas across our concessions safeguard elephants, rhinos and birds.

Water Conservation

Recycling rates above 70%, with closed-loop systems at Jwaneng minimising freshwater draw in the semi-arid Kalahari.

Land Rehabilitation

Progressive rehabilitation returns mined land to productive use — grazing, woodland and habitat for native species.

Renewable Energy

Solar projects underway to power operations with up to 30% renewable energy by 2030.

Community Investment

Schools, clinics, scholarships and SME support across host communities — over P200M invested annually.

Circular Mining

Tailings re-processing extracts more value from less land — cutting waste and extending mine life.
