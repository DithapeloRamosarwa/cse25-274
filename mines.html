Operations Across Botswana

Four World-Class Diamond Mines

FLAGSHIP

Jwaneng Mine

Often called "the world's richest diamond mine by value." Located in the Naledi River Valley, ~120 km west of Gaborone.

Open-pit~12M ct/yr
LARGEST

Orapa Mine

The largest open-pit diamond mine in the world by area. Operating since 1971 in the Boteti district.

Open-pit~10M ct/yr

Letlhakane Mine

One of Botswana's oldest mines (1975). Now focused on tailings re-treatment to extract remaining value.

Tailings~1M ct/yr

Damtshaa Mine

Comprised of four kimberlite pipes near Orapa. Care and maintenance status with future potential.
